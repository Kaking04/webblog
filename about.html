About

The BSCS Educational Tour of 2024 promises an enriching and unforgettable experience for all participants. This year, our journey will take us to three captivating destinations: Manila, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, and Baguio. In Manila, we will explore the bustling capital city, immersing ourselves in its rich history and vibrant culture. Highlights will include visits to historical landmarks, cutting-edge technology hubs, and innovative business centers, providing a well-rounded understanding of the city's role in the country's development.

From Manila, our tour will proceed to the Subic Bay Freeport Zone, where we will delve into the area's unique blend of natural beauty and industrial innovation. Participants will have the opportunity to visit key sites that offer valuable insights into the diverse industries driving this dynamic region. Finally, we will ascend to the cool, picturesque city of Baguio, known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines. In Baguio, we will explore its scenic landscapes, visit educational institutions, and experience its rich cultural heritage. The BSCS Educational Tour of 2024 is designed to provide a comprehensive and engaging learning experience, fostering both personal and professional growth for all attendees.
