Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Day 1/5

On the official first day of our tour, we ventured to the Subic Bay Freeport Zone to immerse ourselves in a variety of educational experiences. Our first stop was the Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center, where we admired the stunning artwork that adorned the venue. The intricate pieces on display ranged from contemporary art to traditional Filipino designs, each telling its own story and showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the region. The center's impressive displays set a creative and inspiring tone for the rest of our day. Next, we visited the PAGASA Weather Station, where we attended an enlightening lecture by one of the operators. We gained valuable insights into the sophisticated technology and processes involved in weather forecasting and monitoring. The operator's passion and expertise were evident as he explained the tools and methods used to track weather patterns, underscoring the importance of accurate weather information in daily life and various industries.

We then proceeded to the First Aviation Academy, where we were thrilled to see multiple aircraft and state-of-the-art flight simulators. The academy provided us with a glimpse into the world of aviation training, showcasing the rigorous training and advanced technology used to prepare future pilots. Our final destination was the Vessel Traffic Management System headquarters. Here, we were greeted by an engineer who oversaw the operations of this complex and beautiful system. Witnessing the coordination and technology involved in managing vessel traffic was incredibly inspiring and highlighted the intricate work behind maritime safety and efficiency.

Day 2/5

On the second day of our tour, we continued our exploration with a visit to QBO Hub, a vibrant center at the heart of innovation in the industry. Here, we witnessed firsthand the diverse activities and support provided to startups, fostering creativity and entrepreneurship. The hub's dynamic environment showcased various initiatives aimed at pushing boundaries and driving forward-thinking solutions across different sectors. It was a valuable insight into the collaborative efforts shaping the future of technology and business in the Philippines.

After a satisfying lunch, our journey led us to Packetworkx Inc. and the IoT Technologies Hub. Here, we delved into their groundbreaking work in network infrastructure and the management of electricity and water systems. We were impressed by their innovative approaches and the integration of cutting-edge technologies to enhance efficiency and sustainability. Learning about their projects underscored the critical role of technology in addressing modern-day challenges and optimizing resource management. This leg of our tour provided a deeper understanding of how these advancements are shaping industries and improving everyday life.

Day 3/5

On the third day of our tour, we embarked on a fascinating visit to the Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA), where we gained insight into the meticulous management and maintenance of their carriages. Inside the maintenance hangar, we observed skilled technicians at work, ensuring the trains operated at peak performance. The control room provided a glimpse into the operational nerve center, where a giant screen displayed real-time data on track conditions and the movement of LRT trains. This experience highlighted the complex infrastructure and dedication required to maintain efficient urban transportation systems.

Following our exploration of the LRTA, we continued to The National Museum of The Philippines, a treasure trove of artistic and cultural heritage. Each art piece, whether sculpted, painted, drawn, or carved, captivated us with its unique beauty and historical significance. The museum's collection spanned various periods and styles, offering a comprehensive journey through Philippine art and history. Notable exhibits included intricate indigenous crafts, masterful colonial-era paintings, and contemporary sculptures reflecting modern Filipino identity. The museum visit enriched our understanding of the country's rich cultural tapestry, showcasing creativity and craftsmanship across generations. It was a profound experience that underscored the enduring legacy and artistic prowess of the Filipino people.

Day 4/5

On the fourth day of our tour, we made our way to Makati to visit 98 Labs, where we were warmly greeted by one of the most impressive CEOs we've encountered. Despite his remarkable skills and achievements, he exuded humility and genuine warmth. He shared his journey from young adulthood to his current role, offering inspiring insights into leadership and entrepreneurship. His personal anecdotes and wisdom left a lasting impression on us, emphasizing the importance of passion and perseverance in achieving success.

Following our inspiring visit to 98 Labs, we proceeded to Hytec Power Inc., a company with a diverse portfolio spanning multiple industries. The office tour was nothing short of astonishing as we explored their impressive array of tools and technologies. From remote-controlled robots used in industrial settings to a VR welding set and simulators featuring construction vehicles, we were immersed in innovation. The hands-on experience allowed us to appreciate the precision and technology behind their operations, highlighting their expertise and versatility across different sectors. It was a truly remarkable experience that showcased the cutting-edge advancements shaping various industries today.

Day 5/5

On the fifth day of our tour, we embarked on an early morning journey to Baguio via buses, starting our day at 1 am. Witnessing the sunrise along the way was a breathtaking sight, painting the sky with hues of orange and pink as we made our way to our first destination. Arriving at the strawberry farm just after dawn, the sunlight cast a warm glow over the fields of strawberries stretching as far as the eye could see. It was a serene and picturesque start to our day, immersing us in the natural beauty of Baguio's countryside.

After exploring the strawberry farm, we continued our adventure to the Chinese Bell Church. Perched atop a hill, the vantage point offered a stunning panoramic view of the city below. The sight of Baguio from this height, with its clusters of buildings and winding roads, was truly awe-inspiring. The tranquil ambiance of the Bell Church added to the experience, offering a moment of reflection and appreciation for the cultural diversity and scenic landscapes of Baguio.

Later in the afternoon, we visited Mines View Park, another iconic destination known for its breathtaking vistas. The hills and mountains bathed in sunlight created a majestic backdrop, showcasing the natural beauty of Baguio's highlands. Beyond the scenic views, Mines View Park offered a variety of attractions including giant hounds, adorned horses, and a vibrant display of flora and fauna native to the region. It was a delightful and immersive experience, allowing us to connect with both the natural and cultural facets of Baguio in a memorable way.
